Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know about shipping, delivery, tracking and receiving your Gemora order.

How long does delivery take? +

Most orders arrive within 3–7 business days, depending on your location and the shipping method selected.

Can I track my shipment? +

Yes. Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive a tracking link via email to monitor its journey.

Is my order insured during transit? +

Every shipment is fully insured until it reaches the delivery address provided at checkout.

Do you offer international shipping? +

We currently ship to selected international destinations. Shipping availability is shown during checkout.

What happens if my package is delayed? +

If a delay occurs, our team will keep you informed and work directly with the carrier to resolve the issue promptly.

Will my order require a signature on delivery? +

Certain high-value orders may require a signature to ensure secure and verified delivery.

Can I change my shipping address after ordering? +

Address changes may be possible before dispatch. Please contact our support team as soon as possible.

How is my jewelry packaged? +

Every piece is presented in our signature packaging, designed to protect your jewelry while creating a memorable unboxing experience.
