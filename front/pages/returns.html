A Simple Return Process

If you need to return an item, we'll guide you through every step of the process to ensure a smooth experience.

01

Contact our team within the return period.

02

Review

We'll review and approve your request.

03

Return

Send the item back in its original packaging.

04

Inspection

Our team verifies the condition of the item.

05

Refund

Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Eligibility

Eligible For Returns

Items may be returned within 7 days of delivery provided they are unused, unworn and returned in their original packaging with all tags and certificates included.

Exclusions

Non-Returnable Items

  • — Customized or engraved jewellery
  • — Final sale items
  • — Worn or damaged products
  • — Gift cards and promotional items
Refunds

Refund Processing

Once your returned item passes inspection, refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank.

Support

Damaged Or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, defective or incorrect, please contact us within 48 hours and include photographs of the item and packaging.
