Last Updated: June 2026

Eligibility for Returns

Items may be returned within 7 days of delivery provided they are unused, unworn, and returned in their original packaging with all tags and certificates intact.

Non-Returnable Items

  • • Customized or engraved jewellery
  • • Final sale items
  • • Products showing signs of wear or damage
  • • Gift cards and promotional items

Return Process

To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number. Once approved, instructions for shipping the product back will be provided. Returned items are inspected before a refund is processed.

Refunds

After successful inspection of the returned item, refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery along with photographs of the item and packaging so that we can resolve the issue promptly.

Need Assistance?

Our support team is available to help with returns, exchanges, refunds, and order-related questions.
