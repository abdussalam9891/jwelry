Caring For Your Jewellery

Last Updated: June 2026

Gemora jewellery is crafted to be cherished for years. Proper care and storage will help preserve its beauty, shine, and longevity.

Warranty Coverage

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Craftsmanship issues
  • Structural faults

Jewellery Care

  • Store separately
  • Avoid chemicals
  • Clean with a soft cloth
  • Remove before swimming

Warranty Coverage

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects identified under normal use. If a defect is confirmed, we may repair or replace the item at our discretion.

What Is Not Covered

  • • Normal wear and tear
  • • Accidental damage
  • • Improper storage or handling
  • • Unauthorized repairs or alterations
  • • Loss or theft

Jewellery Care Tips

  • • Store pieces separately to prevent scratches.
  • • Avoid direct contact with perfumes and cosmetics.
  • • Remove jewellery before swimming or exercising.
  • • Keep jewellery away from harsh chemicals.
  • • Clean gently using a soft polishing cloth.

Repairs & Maintenance

If your jewellery requires inspection, repair, or maintenance, contact our support team. We will guide you through the evaluation process and available service options.

Warranty Claims

To submit a warranty request, please provide your order number, photographs of the product, and a brief description of the issue.
